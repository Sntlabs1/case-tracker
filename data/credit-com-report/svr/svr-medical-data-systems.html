Executive summary

Across a 5,528,402-consumer national credit panel, Medical Data Systems furnished 298,976 tradelines to 83,618 distinct consumers. Of those tradelines, 44.4% were marked disputed yet continued to report — a population-wide pattern of furnishing after dispute that no single-plaintiff record set can establish.

The systemic signal: 57,399 consumers disputed a Medical Data Systems tradeline; 124,132 of those disputed tradelines were still reporting a positive balance. 1,153 consumers have a Medical Data Systems tradeline reported on/after 2024 — the violation is current, not historical.

Recovery exposure (illustrative ceiling)

FCRA § 1681n provides statutory damages of $100–$1,000 per willful violation. Applied to the 57,399 consumers with a disputed Medical Data Systems tradeline, statutory exposure ranges $5.7M – $57.4M, before actual or punitive damages and fees. A ceiling for scoping, not an expected recovery.
