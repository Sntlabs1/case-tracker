Executive summary

Across a 5,528,402-consumer national credit panel, Commonwealth Financial Systems furnished 358,313 tradelines to 147,497 distinct consumers. Of those tradelines, 40.9% were marked disputed yet continued to report — a population-wide pattern of furnishing after dispute that no single-plaintiff record set can establish.

The systemic signal: 85,628 consumers disputed a Commonwealth Financial Systems tradeline; 146,291 of those disputed tradelines were still reporting a positive balance. 111 consumers have a Commonwealth Financial Systems tradeline reported on/after 2024 — the violation is current, not historical.

Recovery exposure (illustrative ceiling)

FCRA § 1681n provides statutory damages of $100–$1,000 per willful violation. Applied to the 85,628 consumers with a disputed Commonwealth Financial Systems tradeline, statutory exposure ranges $8.6M – $85.6M, before actual or punitive damages and fees. A ceiling for scoping, not an expected recovery.
